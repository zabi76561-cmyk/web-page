Our Story

School has been a beacon of education since its founding in 1988. Our commitment to mold needy students with academic excellence and moral standards of life has enabled them to grow from strength to strength, preparing them to face global and professional challenges.

Ever since its inception, School has survived amidst formidable obstacles. There are many stories that convey the challenges and inspiring moments in respect of government permissions and language policy. But today, our institute enjoys an enviable reputation for its humanitarian service and God's faithfulness towards his people. We love God and thereby carry His message to everyone, rather than seeking any benefit.

Today, the education system is totally revamping with new challenges. Let us pray that God Almighty keeps us true towards our motto, mission, and commitment.

Teaching Strategies to Help Students Succeed

The main priority of any teacher is to ensure that their students succeed. A student's success can be measured in many ways: by receiving good grades, how much they are involved in class, or even how well they have improved.

To make education for all - The right to education is not only the right to access education but also the right to receive an education of good quality. Education must be available and accessible but also acceptable and adaptable.

Message To Parents

Your children are precious to you and to us. Your co-operation is indispensable for the effective education of your children. Hence you are expected to look into the diary regularly for perfect observance of school regulations and performance of the given homework. Co-operation between parents and teachers will give better results.

The entire responsibility of bringing the children to school and taking them home lies with the parents. Therefore, you are advised to carefully select the transportation or attendants who bring your children to and from school.
